"Education is a Gift for Life"

From the smallest preschooler to the tallest teenager, our mission is to prepare students for success in college and career.

Our Impact

Thanks to the help and support of our School Board, the City, business leaders, parents, teachers, and the community, the Lynwood Unified School District has:

Increased Graduation Rates
Increased high school graduation rates from 67% to 81% at Lynwood High School and from 69% to 90% at Firebaugh HighSchool
Lowered Dropout Rates
 Lowered the dropout rate from 32% to 18%
Raised Academics
Raised Academic Performance Indicators (APIs) by 34 points from 689 to 723, which includes an 18-point rise for AfricanAmerican students, a 12-point rise for Latino students, and a 32-point rise for students with disabilities.
