About LPEF

Lynwood Partners Educational Foundation is where business leaders, donors, community leaders, distinguished alumni, and educators join together to invest in Lynwood’s public schools. We want Lynwood’s public schools to be the crown jewel of the community because Education is a Gift for Life.

EDUCATIONAL SUCCESS
Through our Achieving Greatness initiative, high school graduation rates have risen; from 67% to 81% at Lynwood High School and 69% to 90% at Firebaugh High School. The drop-out rate has been slashed from 32% to 18%, and the District’s Academic Performance Indicators—California’s official measure of academic progress are the fastest rising in the county and the state.

FINANCIAL SUCCESS
In 2013-14, California instituted the new Local Control Funding Formula, the most comprehensive reform to school funding in 40 years, and it is a huge win for Lynwood. By 2018, it restores school funding to pre-recession levels and provides additional funding for high needs students. Each district must create a local accountability plan showing parents and the community how funds are being invested and demonstrate progress on college and career preparedness, student engagement, and parental involvement. In 2012, Lynwood residents approved Measure K which provides $93 million for capital improvements. It was the first school bond measure to pass in 10 years and a strong vote of confidence from parents and the community. BUILDING ON

SUCCESS
Though much has been accomplished, so much more remains to be done. Funding lost in the recession and decades of deferred maintenance have left Lynwood schools with a deep technology deficit, obsolete libraries, minimal arts education, and inadequate sports facilities and equipment. Help us provide students with the skills, abilities and tools necessary to be successful in 21st century jobs and careers. We invite you to invest in success by joining the Lynwood Partners Educational Foundation… because Education is a Gift for Life.
